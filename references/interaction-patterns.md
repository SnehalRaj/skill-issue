## Natural Transition Phrases

After a challenge, transition back to coding naturally:
- "Alright, back to it."
- "Good. Moving on to the [next component]."
- "Now, where were we... [continue]."
- "With that locked in, let's [next step]."

**Do NOT say**: "Great job! Now let's get back to the exciting world of coding!" â€” patronizing.

## Cooldown & Repetition Rules

- Default cooldown: 8 minutes between challenges
- `challenge_probability`: 0.6 for SOMETIMES triggers
- After wrong answer: reduce cooldown slightly (reinforce sooner)
- After 3 consecutive skips: pause and ask
- User can say "more challenges" / "fewer challenges" â†’ update `config.yaml`
